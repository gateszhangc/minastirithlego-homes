The Set

LEGO® Icons The Lord of the Rings: Minas Tirith™

Embark on an epic building journey with the most ambitious LEGO® Icons set inspired by The Lord of the Rings™ trilogy. This stunning 8,278-piece model recreates the seven levels of the White City of Gondor with remarkable precision and attention to detail.

The set features a hybrid-scale design that combines a sweeping microscale cityscape with intricate minifigure-scale interior details, including the throne room of the Citadel, the Court of the Fountain, and the iconic White Tree of Gondor.

Set Number 11377
Pieces 8,278
Minifigures 10

Craftsmanship

A Monumental Building Experience

Designed for adult builders and Lord of the Rings™ enthusiasts, this set offers weeks of immersive construction. Every level of Minas Tirith is faithfully reproduced, from the Great Gate to the Citadel at the summit.

Features

Every Detail of the White City

From architecture to minifigures, every element has been carefully crafted to bring Minas Tirith to life.

Hybrid-Scale Design

A unique building approach that combines a sweeping microscale cityscape with detailed minifigure-scale interior spaces, offering the best of both worlds.

10 Minifigures

Includes Gandalf the White, Aragorn as King Elessar, Faramir, Denethor, Peregrin Took, Arwen, 4 Soldiers of Gondor, plus a Shadowfax horse figure.

Authentic Architecture

Faithfully recreates the seven levels of Minas Tirith with the Great Gate, paved courtyards, towering spires, and the iconic White Tree of Gondor.

Premium Display Piece

Measuring over 16 in. (41 cm) high, 18 in. (46 cm) wide, and 12 in. (31 cm) deep, this set makes a spectacular centerpiece for any collection.

Immersive Building Journey

With 8,278 pieces spread across numbered bags, this set delivers weeks of rewarding construction, perfect for evenings dedicated to the craft.

25th Anniversary Edition

Celebrating 25 years of The Lord of the Rings film trilogy, this special commemorative set features exclusive details and premium packaging.
